Company email can be added to Outlook automatically on managed laptops; simply open Outlook and sign in with your company email address, and the configuration is applied without manual server settings.

On mobile phones, install the Outlook mobile app from the app store and sign in the same way. Manual IMAP or POP configuration is not supported and will not connect correctly.

To request access to a shared team mailbox, submit an access request ticket naming the mailbox and your manager for approval; access is normally granted within one business day.
