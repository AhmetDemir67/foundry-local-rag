To reset your company password, go to the self-service portal at password.company.local and click "Forgot Password". You will need to verify your identity with your employee ID and a code sent to your registered phone number.

Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character. Passwords expire every 90 days, and the system will remind you one week before expiration.

If you are locked out after too many failed attempts, the self-service portal cannot help you. In that case, contact the IT Helpdesk at extension 4357 (HELP) to have your account manually unlocked.
