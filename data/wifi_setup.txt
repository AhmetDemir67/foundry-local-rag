To connect to the corporate Wi-Fi network "CorpNet-Secure", select it from your Wi-Fi list and sign in with your regular company username and password. Your device must already be enrolled in device management for this network to accept the connection.

Guests and personal devices should use "CorpNet-Guest" instead, which requires a temporary code obtainable from the front desk and does not have access to internal systems.

If CorpNet-Secure repeatedly rejects your credentials even though they are correct, your device certificate may have expired; this requires the IT Helpdesk to re-enroll the device.
